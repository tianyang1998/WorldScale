# Privacy Policy — World Scale Desktop

**Last Revised: April 27, 2026**

---

## 1. Introduction

World Scale Desktop ("we", "us", "our") is a standalone desktop multiplayer RPG where your real-world professional credentials determine your character's power. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights over it.

This desktop application maintains a separate player database from the World Scale web version. Accounts created here are independent of any web account.

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at worldscaleplate@gmail.com.

---

## 2. What We Collect

- **Email address** — provided when you create an account or sign in.
- **Professional credentials** — the data you enter to score your character (e.g. h-index, GitHub statistics, years of practice, publications, notable cases). This data is voluntary and entered by you. We do not independently verify it.
- **Character data** — your character name, realm, tier, gold, stats, and equipped cosmetics, generated from your credentials.
- **Technical data** — our database provider (Supabase) may automatically collect technical information such as IP addresses and request timestamps as part of normal infrastructure operation. The desktop application itself does not collect crash reports, usage telemetry, or analytics.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

- To authenticate you and maintain your session.
- To calculate your character's power score from your credentials.
- To run multiplayer features: world map presence, PvP battles, and PvE raids.
- To display your rank on the public leaderboard.

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell your data to any third party. We do not "share" your data with third parties for cross-context behavioral advertising as defined under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and similar state laws. Your credentials and character data are processed solely by Supabase as described in §4, for the purpose of operating the game.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

- **Supabase** — provides authentication, database storage, and real-time multiplayer infrastructure. Data may be stored in EU or US data centers. [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy).

No other third-party services have access to your personal data. The desktop application does not contact any analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting services.

---

## 5. Data Retention

Your account and character data is retained until you delete your account. Upon deletion, your data is permanently removed from our database. Infrastructure logs held by Supabase may be retained subject to their own policies.

---

## 6. Your Rights

Regardless of where you are located, you have the following rights:

- **Access** — request a copy of the data we hold about you by emailing worldscaleplate@gmail.com.
- **Correction** — update your professional credentials at any time via the credential entry screen in the application.
- **Deletion** — permanently delete your account and all associated data by contacting worldscaleplate@gmail.com.
- **Portability** — request your data in a portable format by emailing worldscaleplate@gmail.com.
- **Opt-Out Signals (GPC)** — We honor Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals as a valid opt-out of data sharing for advertising or cross-context behavioral tracking. Because we do not engage in such sharing, a GPC signal has no practical effect on how we process your data — but we recognize and respect it as required by California, Colorado, Connecticut, and other applicable state laws.

---

## 7. Children & Sensitive Data

World Scale Desktop is intended for working professionals and is not designed for users under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 16. If you believe a minor has provided us with their data, please contact us at worldscaleplate@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

The professional credentials you enter (such as publication counts, GitHub statistics, or years of practice) are not "sensitive personal data" as defined under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or CCPA. Sensitive categories under those laws include health records, biometric data, racial or ethnic origin, financial account details, and similar categories. Aggregate professional metrics do not fall within these categories.

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Revised" date at the top of this document. Significant changes will be surfaced in the application at next launch. Your continued use of World Scale Desktop after any change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
